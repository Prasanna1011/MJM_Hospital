About Us

We are a multispeciality hospital located in the heart of Pune. Our motto is to provide care with utmost concern for our patients. Our commitment to delivering quality care to improve our patients’ daily lives has earned our organization several accolades in media and the industry. Our staff and doctors ensure the highest protocol and are extremely experienced in their field. We use industry-standard equipment and technology to deliver exceptional customer satisfaction.
